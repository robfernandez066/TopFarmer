Preserve camera zoom/pan geometry, crop lifecycle, plot hit rectangle, approved `0.97` scale, approved soft-click asset and import settings, plot/crop assets, balance data, portrait/project settings, Android preset contract, and every unrelated behavior
- Make the farm-camera input path the authoritative touch-gesture classifier without giving it crop state, crop selection, clock access, or plant/harvest logic. FarmPlot may receive a confirmed tap/cancel instruction but cannot independently produce touch feedback before classification
- A first touch landing on a plot records only a possible tap: before release it produces no plot scale change, no click playback, no activation signal, and no crop mutation, regardless of how long that finger is held
- Confirm a touch as a tap only when that same finger releases over the same plot, its total displacement never exceeded `12` logical pixels, and no second finger appeared at any time during the gesture. - Crossing the existing pan threshold, releasing outside the original plot, touch cancellation, focus loss, visibility loss, or scene exit discards the pending tap with zero feedback/action and restores exact scale `1.0`. A new camera gesture that begins while a prior confirmed-tap pulse is still returning cancels that visual pulse and restores `1.0` before processing the camera gesture, without undoing the already confirmed tap
- Extra fingers cannot become a tap candidate, change the active pinch pair, trigger feedback, or leave input/scale stuck. After every gesture fully ends, the next ordinary tap must work on its first attempt
- Preserve camera behavior from TASK-025 exactly: default centered zoom `1.0`, limits `1.0..2.0`, midpoint-anchored proportional pinch, same-direction pan above zoom `1.0`, stable edge clamps, no inertia/overscroll/gray exposure, `0.1` pointer-anchored wheel steps, and middle-button pan
- Apply the same classification to editor left mouse: press alone is silent and unscaled; release over the same plot without disqualifying movement produces one `0.10`-second pulse, one click, and at most one activation. Mouse wheel and middle-button camera gestures never trigger plot feedback
- Update existing regressions and add one focused deterministic regression that covers silent touch-down/hold, confirmed tap timing and anchoring, slow and fast pinch-in/out beginning on each plot, second-finger arrival after a held first touch, stationary two-finger contact, pan from a plot, threshold boundary, outside release, extra fingers, focus/visibility/scene cancellation, pulse interruption by a new camera gesture, first tap after each cancellation, mouse parity, and no gameplay mutation from camera gestures.
